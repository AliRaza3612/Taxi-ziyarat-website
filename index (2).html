About Us

Your Trusted Guide in the
Holy Land

Assalamu Alaikum! I am Shahzad, a professional taxi driver and ziyarat guide based in Makkah Al-Mukarramah. I have been serving pilgrims from around the world for over a decade, offering comfortable, reliable, and spiritually enriching tours of Islam's most sacred sites.

Whether you are performing Umrah, Hajj, or simply visiting the blessed cities, I will ensure your journey is safe, comfortable, and deeply meaningful. My vehicle is air-conditioned and I speak Urdu, Arabic, and English.

AC Comfortable Vehicle

Travel in comfort with our clean, air-conditioned private vehicle at all times.

Knowledgeable Guide

Deep knowledge of Islamic history and significance of every holy site you visit.

24/7 Availability

Available anytime — day or night — to accommodate your schedule and prayer times.

Holy Sites

Sacred Destinations We Cover

Masjid al-Haram

The Grand Mosque surrounding the Holy Kaaba

Jabal an-Nour (Cave Hira)

Where the first revelation was revealed to Prophet ﷺ

Jabal Thawr

Cave where the Prophet ﷺ took refuge during Hijra

Mina

The tent city — site of Rami al-Jamarat during Hajj

Arafat (Jabal ar-Rahmah)

The Hill of Mercy — central rite of Hajj

Muzdalifah

Sacred plain for overnight stay during Hajj

Masjid al-Ji'ranah

Mosque where the Prophet ﷺ assumed Ihram

Masjid al-Hudaibiyah

Site of the famous Treaty of Hudaibiyah

Masjid an-Nabawi

The Prophet's Mosque — resting place of Prophet ﷺ

Masjid Quba

First mosque ever built in Islamic history

Masjid al-Qiblatayn

Mosque of the Two Qiblas — historic direction change

Uhud Mountain

Site of the Battle of Uhud and grave of Hamza RA

Al-Baqi Cemetery

Sacred graveyard of the Prophet's ﷺ companions

Masjid al-Ghamama

Where the Prophet ﷺ performed Eid prayers

Masjid al-Khandaq

Site of the Battle of the Trench

Dates Market (Souk)

Famous date market with varieties from around Arabia
